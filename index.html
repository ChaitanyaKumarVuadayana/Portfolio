Smart Mining Safety Helmet

The Smart Safety Mining Helmet is an innovative and advanced solution designed to revolutionize safety practices in mining operations. It is a state-of-the-art wearable device that integrates cutting-edge technologies to monitor workers' health, prevent accidents, and provide immediate assistance in case of emergencies. This smart helmet aims to ensure the well-being and security of mining personnel, making mining operations safer and more efficient.

Smart Street Light System

Smart Street Light System is an energy-efficient solution for highways that utilizes infrared sensors to detect vehicle movement. Lights remain off during inactivity, reducing power consumption. When a vehicle passes, the infrared sensors trigger the lights to illuminate, ensuring optimal safety and minimal energy wastage. This smart system promotes sustainable practices and enhances highway safety. Apply for patents from collage.

Skin Cancer Detection

The Skin Cancer Detection Algorithm is a powerful machine learning solution that utilizes Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) to identify skin cancer from medical images. Trained on a dataset sourced from Kaggle, this innovative algorithm automatically learns intricate patterns and features indicative of skin cancer. By leveraging deep learning capabilities, it achieves high accuracy in distinguishing between benign and malignant skin lesions. The algorithm holds tremendous potential to aid dermatologists and healthcare professionals in early detection and improved management of skin cancer, ultimately contributing to better patient outcomes and saving lives.
